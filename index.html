Top Five Movies According To Md Munna

My top five Movies of All-Time

  1. 3 Idiots

    • A comedy-drama that challenges conventional education systems and
      societal pressures, advocating for pursuing one's passion and
      individuality over societal expectations

  2. Taare Zameen Par

    • A touching drama highlighting the struggles of a dyslexic child and
      advocating for a more inclusive education system that recognizes and
      nurtures individual talents and abilities.

  3. Gully Boy

    • Groundbreaking portrayal of the underground rap scene in Mumbai,
      shedding light on social and economic issues through powerful
      storytelling and music.

  4. Lagaan: Once Upon a Time in India

    • A period drama about a group of villagers who challenge British
      colonial oppression through a cricket match, showcasing the power of
      unity and determination against adversity.

  5. Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (DDLJ)

    • DDLJ is a timeless love story that revolutionized Bollywood by
      redefining romance and family dynamics, becoming an enduring
      cultural icon beloved by generations.
